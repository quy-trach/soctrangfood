Banh Cong (Bánh Cống)
Bong cake is a dish in Soc Trang province, Vietnam, a specialty of the Khmer people in the South. The cake is crispy and the way to make it is to put fried dough and shrimp in the mold like the Ho Tay shrimp cake of Hanoi people. The cake has a slightly dark color, not as fresh as other places, but it is fragrant and extremely attractive.
